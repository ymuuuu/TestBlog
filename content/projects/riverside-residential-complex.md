## Project Overview

The Riverside Residential Complex brings luxury waterfront living to Seattle's revitalized Riverside District. This thoughtfully designed community features 150 modern residential units across three mid-rise buildings, offering residents stunning river views, resort-style amenities, and a vibrant community atmosphere.

## Project Scope

### Residential Buildings
- Three 8-story buildings with elevator access
- Mix of 1, 2, and 3-bedroom units
- Private balconies or terraces for every unit
- Floor-to-ceiling windows maximizing natural light
- High-end finishes and smart home technology

### Community Amenities
- Clubhouse with resident lounge and co-working spaces
- Resort-style pool and spa
- Fitness center with yoga studio
- Children's playground and splash pad
- Dog park and pet washing station
- Community gardens with raised beds
- Riverside walking trails and bike paths

### Parking & Infrastructure
- Two-level underground parking garage
- EV charging stations
- Secure bicycle storage
- Package receiving center
- On-site maintenance facilities

## Design Philosophy

Our approach focused on creating a true community rather than just housing:

- **Connection to Nature**: Extensive landscaping and direct river access
- **Indoor-Outdoor Living**: Large balconies and outdoor gathering spaces
- **Sustainability**: Energy-efficient design and green building practices
- **Community**: Shared spaces that encourage neighbor interaction
- **Accessibility**: Universal design principles throughout

## Construction Challenges & Solutions

### Riverfront Construction
Building near water presented unique challenges:
- Installed erosion control and siltation barriers
- Designed specialized foundation systems for variable soil conditions
- Coordinated with environmental agencies for wildlife protection
- Implemented strict stormwater management protocols

### Urban Integration
- Maintained access to adjacent properties during construction
- Coordinated with city for street improvements
- Phased construction to minimize community disruption
- Created temporary pedestrian pathways

## Sustainability Features

- **Energy**: 30% more efficient than code requirements
- **Water**: Low-flow fixtures and drought-tolerant landscaping
- **Materials**: Locally sourced materials reducing transportation impact
- **Lighting**: LED lighting throughout with automatic controls
- **Waste**: 80% construction waste recycled or reused

## Technical Details

- **Total Units**: 150 residential units

- **Total Building Area**: 280,000 sq ft residential + 15,000 sq ft amenities
- **Parking Spaces**: 220 underground spaces
